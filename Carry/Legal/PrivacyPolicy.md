# Privacy Policy

**Carry — Golf Skins Tracker**
**Last Updated: March 2, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Carry ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. Apple's "Hide My Email" feature is fully supported.
- **Profile Information**: Name, display initials, avatar selection, color preference.
- **Golf Information**: Handicap index, GHIN number (optional), scores, round history.
- **Game Data**: Groups, rounds, skins results, and leaderboard statistics.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type and operating system version, used solely for app compatibility.
- **Usage Data**: Basic app interaction data to improve performance and fix bugs.

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect precise location data
- We do not collect contacts or address book information
- We do not collect financial or payment information
- We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics
- We do not collect health or fitness data

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Display your profile and scores to other players in your rounds
- Calculate handicaps, skins, and game results
- Improve and optimize the App experience

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with:
- Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL)
- Row-level security (RLS) policies ensuring users can only access authorized data
- Data hosted in secure cloud infrastructure

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your game data (scores, skins results) is visible to other players in your rounds and groups. This is core to the App's functionality.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

## 7. Third-Party Services

- **Apple Sign-In**: Authentication is handled by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy for their data practices.
- **Supabase**: Backend data storage. See Supabase's Privacy Policy at supabase.com/privacy.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Carry is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

## 11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by other means.

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights:
