# Privacy Policy

**Carry — Golf Skins Tracker**
**Effective Date: March 22, 2026**

Diverse Awareness Inc. ("Carry," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Carry mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the App. By using Carry, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you sign in using Apple Sign-In, we receive your name and email address (or a private relay email address if you choose to hide your email).
- **Profile Information**: Your display name, avatar photo (captured via camera or selected from photo library), home golf club, club membership status (member or non-member), and GHIN handicap number (optional).
- **Golf Scores**: Scores you enter for each hole during skins games, including handicap index for net scoring.
- **Group Membership**: Information about the player groups you create or join, including group names, player associations, tee times, buy-in amounts, and game recurrence settings.
- **Course Preferences**: Golf courses you select or save for your games.
- **SMS Invitations**: When you invite players via SMS, the App opens the native SMS composer on your device. We do not access, store, or transmit phone numbers or contact data; the invitation is sent directly from your device's messaging app.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers necessary for app functionality.
- **Usage Data**: Basic information about how you interact with the App, such as features used and session duration, to help us improve the experience.
- **Push Notifications**: If you opt in, we use Apple Push Notification service (APNs) to send game-started alerts, group invite notifications, round completion updates, and scorer assignment changes. Your device token is stored securely on our Supabase servers solely to deliver these notifications. We do not use notifications for marketing or advertising purposes. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.
- **Locally Cached Data**: Scores and game state are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults and local storage to support offline functionality. This data syncs to the cloud when an internet connection is available.

### Information We Do Not Collect
- We do not collect payment information, financial account details, or transaction data. Venmo deep links open the Venmo app externally; no financial data passes through Carry.
- We do not collect precise location data.
- We do not access your contacts, phone book, or call history.
- We do not use cookies or web-based tracking technologies within the App.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Scorekeeping & Live Syncing**: To record, display, and sync golf scores across groups in real time during skins games (approximately every 15 seconds when online).
- **Leaderboards & Statistics**: To calculate and display skins results, player standings, season statistics, and all-time leaderboards.
- **Group Management**: To enable you to create, join, and manage player groups, set tee times, and configure recurring games.
- **Subscriptions**: To manage your subscription status and determine which features are available to you. Subscription billing is handled entirely by Apple; we do not collect or store payment information.
- **Notifications**: To send local tee time reminders, game-started alerts, and skin-won notifications to your device when you have opted in.
- **Offline Support**: To store scores locally on your device so the App functions without an internet connection, and to automatically sync data when connectivity returns.
- **Profile & Handicap**: To display your profile information to other players in your groups and to calculate net handicap-adjusted scoring.
- **App Improvement**: To understand how the App is used and to improve its features and performance.
- **Communication**: To respond to your support requests or inquiries.
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

## 3. Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.**

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

- **With Other Players**: Your name, scores, and group membership are visible to other players in your skins games, as this is essential to the App's functionality.
- **Service Providers**: We use third-party services that process data on our behalf (see Section 4).
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, in response to a valid legal process, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Carry uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Supabase** | Backend database and authentication | Account info, profile data, scores, group data, avatar photos |
| **Apple Sign-In** | User authentication | Name, email (per Apple's privacy controls) |
| **Golf Course API** | Course data (names, holes, par, slope/rating) | Course search queries only — no personal data |
| **Apple Push Notification service (APNs)** | Push notifications for game events | Device token, notification content |
| **Venmo** | Deep link only (not integrated) | None — opens Venmo app externally |

Supabase hosts our database infrastructure in the United States. Your data is stored with encryption at rest and in transit, protected by row-level security policies that ensure users can only access data within their own groups. For more information about Supabase's data practices, please visit [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).

The Golf Course API is used solely to retrieve course information (course names, hole data, par, slope, and rating). No personal user data is shared with this service.

Venmo links are provided solely as a convenience for settling up between players. Tapping a Venmo link opens the Venmo app outside of Carry with a pre-filled amount and recipient. We do not send any personal or financial data to Venmo and have no integration with or affiliation with Venmo or PayPal. No money flows through Carry.

## 5. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored in two locations:

- **Cloud (Supabase)**: Account information, profile data, scores, group data, and avatar photos are stored securely on Supabase servers in the United States.
- **Local Device (UserDefaults)**: Scores, game state, and cached data are stored locally on your iPhone to support offline functionality and faster load times. This data is deleted when you uninstall the App.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. Specifically:

- **Account data**: Retained while your account exists.
- **Game and score data**: Retained to provide game history, season statistics, and leaderboard results.
- **Avatar photos**: Stored in cloud storage while your account exists. Deleted upon account deletion.
- **Deleted accounts**: Upon account deletion, your personal data will be removed from our active systems within 30 days. Some data may be retained in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being permanently deleted.

## 6. Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time through the account deletion option in the App or by contacting us at support@carryapp.site. Upon receiving a verified deletion request, we will:

- Delete your account and profile information.
- Remove your personal data from our active database.
- Anonymize or delete your scores and game history.

Deletion will be completed within 30 days of the request. We may retain certain data as required by law.

## 7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.
- Secure authentication via Apple Sign-In.
- Row-level security policies on our database to ensure users can only access their own data.
- Regular review of our data security practices.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Children's Privacy

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a person under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@carryapp.site.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

The Carry mobile application does not use cookies, web beacons, or similar tracking technologies. We do not track you across other apps or websites. We do not participate in cross-app advertising or tracking frameworks.
