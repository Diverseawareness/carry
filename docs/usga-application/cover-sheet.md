## Brief Summary for Email Submission

> Carry is an iOS app for tracking competitive "skins" golf games between friends and club members. It uses handicap-adjusted net scoring and supports multi-group rounds of up to 20 players. We are applying for GHIN API access to provide users with accurate, up-to-date Handicap Indices instead of manually-entered values that drift out of date and create disputes in money games.
>
> Our specific GHIN data uses:
> - Handicap Index (fetched on profile setup and manual refresh)
> - Course / slope rating (for stroke allocation)
> - Golfer profile verification (confirm GHIN number ownership)
> - Score posting (post-launch, user-initiated only)
>
> We have published Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, and we've documented our security architecture (see attached). The app is in TestFlight beta and targeting App Store launch within 30 days.
>
> Happy to discuss further or provide additional documentation.
