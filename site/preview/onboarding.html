The model: pre-fill skips screens

1

Bob's bulk-invite is the prerequisite. He enters name, handicap, phone for each invitee in iOS. That metadata rides into the deep link as invite_token.

2

Tap link → app fetches pre-fill via resolve_invite_token. Whatever Bob filled, we silently write into the new profiles row after Apple Sign-In.

3

Magical case = 3 of our screens. Invite landing → Apple sheet → paywall → home. No typing. Under 30 seconds, glove on.

4

Missing data inserts a screen. No phone → phone confirm. No name/HC → profile confirm. No invite token at all → original 5-screen flow.

5

Apple Sign-In and the paywall stay. Apple is one Face ID tap; paywall has to happen before Friday morning so it doesn't block the round on the tee box.

6

Edit-later for accuracy. If Bob misspelled a name or got an index wrong, the invitee fixes it in Profile → Edit. Not blocking first run.
