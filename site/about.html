The story behind Carry.

The Problem

Every Friday at my club, the same thing happens. Twelve guys show up for skins. Someone pulls out their phone to track scores in the Notes app. Someone else texts the group chat asking who won hole 7. By the 18th hole, nobody agrees on the payouts.

We've been playing skins for years, and every week it's the same chaos. Scraps of paper, mental math, arguments about carries. There had to be a better way.

The Solution

Carry is the app I wished existed. Set up your skins game in seconds, assign tee times and groups, invite players via SMS, and let everyone follow the action live from their phone. Scores sync across all groups every 15 seconds. No more guessing who's winning. No more end-of-round debates about who owes what.

The app handles carries, tracks every skin won, calculates payouts instantly, and sends push notifications when someone wins a skin. It even works offline on the course.

About Me

I'm Daniel, the founder of Carry. I'm a member at a club in the East Bay, Bay Area, where skins games are a way of life. I built Carry because I was tired of the friction — setting up games via group texts, tracking scores on napkins, and spending 20 minutes after the round figuring out who won what.

Carry started as a tool for my own Friday game and grew into something I think every skins group needs. Whether you play at a private club, a muni, or a resort course on vacation — if you play skins, Carry is for you.

What's In The App

Carry now includes cross-group live score syncing, handicap-adjusted net and gross scoring, carries support, offline mode, push notifications for tee times and skin wins, recurring game scheduling, SMS invites, Quick Games, guest player profiles, avatar photos, home club designation, and leaderboards. Carry Premium ($29.99/year or $4.99/month, with a 7-day free trial) unlocks unlimited skins groups, full round history, and all-time stats.

Quick Games let you start a skins game in seconds. The creator sets up the game, adds players, picks a course, and invites a scorer for each group — the only players who need the app. You get 3 free Quick Games per month, or unlimited with Premium.

I'd love to hear from you. Whether it's a feature request, a bug report, or just to tell me about your skins game — your feedback shapes what gets built next. Reach out anytime at support@carryapp.site.
